(1) What is the independent variable? What is the dependent variable?

In this case the independent variable is the congruence and the time taken to recongnize the word is the dependent variable. This is because time is dependent on whether the words are congruent or incongruent

(2) What is an appropriate set of hypotheses for this task? What kind of statistical test do you expect to perform? Justify your choices.

The null hypothesis is that there is no significant difference between the time it takes the particinpants to name the color, in other words the reaction time for both congruent and incongruent are similar. The alternative hypothesis is that there is a significant difference in reaction times for the participants between congruent and incongruent words. The statisitcal test I will perform is a dependent two tailed t-test with significance 0.05. The test will allow us to better understand the difference between reaction times of both congruent and incongruent words and will prove whether there is a significant difference between the two. This is a paired t-test because the same participant is being tested under two different conditions. T-test will be the best choice in this experiment because population parameters are unknown, and the sample size is less than 30.
